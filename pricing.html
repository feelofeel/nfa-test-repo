Transparent & Fair Pricing

Custom Quotes for Your Specific Needs

At ProShield Roofing, we believe in providing fair, transparent, and accurate pricing. Because every roof is unique, we don't offer one-size-fits-all prices. Instead, we provide a detailed, custom quote after a thorough, on-site inspection.

Factors That Influence Cost

Several factors determine the final cost of a roofing project, including:

  • The size and complexity of your roof
  • The type of materials you choose (e.g., asphalt shingles, metal, tile)
  • The extent of any existing damage
  • The slope and accessibility of your roof
  • Local building code requirements
